## About

I design and build practical systems that connect business processes, software, APIs, databases, automation tools, and applied AI.

My work focuses on turning scattered operations into clear workflows: capturing information, organizing it, assigning ownership, triggering next actions, and giving teams better visibility into what is happening.

I care about systems that are useful, maintainable, and grounded in how people actually work.

## How I work

- Understand the business process before choosing the tool.
- Clarify ownership, inputs, status, and next actions before automating.
- Prefer connected systems over isolated tools.
- Build small, usable systems before adding complexity.
- Document architecture, trade-offs, and operational decisions clearly.
